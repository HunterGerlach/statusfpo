Infrastructure 2020 Datacentre Moves

During the time frame of 2020-04-13 to 2020-07-01, all Fedora Infrastructure servers will be moved at different times.

This page gives information about when what boxes will be down, and when they are finished moving.

During this time different sets of systems will be down or in a degraded mode for multiple weeks at a time.

Week 1 (Communishift) 2020-04-13
  • Powering down of communishift
  • Powering down of select systems in production
  • Packing of systems and shipping to NC Datacentre
Week 2 (Communishift) 2020-04-20
  • Shipment arrives in RDU
  • Shipment arrvies in IAD
  • Racks are set up in RDU
Week 3 (Communishift) 2020-04-27
  • Setup of Communishift systems in RDU
  • Begin work on Minimum Viable Fedora (MVF) in IAD
Week 4 (IAD) 2020-05-04
  • Work on MVF continues
Week 5 (IAD) 2020-05-11
  • Work on MVF continues
Week 6 (IAD) 2020-05-18
  • Work on MVF continues
Week 7 (IAD) 2020-05-25
  • Switch over to MVF from PHX datacentre
Week 8 (PHX) 2020-06-01
  • Teardown and packing of PHX datacentre
Week 9 (PHX/IAD) 2020-06-08
  • shipment of systems across country
Week 10 (IAD) 2020-06-15
  • add services to MVF to bring up to fully functional
Week 11 (IAD) 2020-06-22
  • add services to MVF to bring up to fully functional
Week 12 (IAD) 2020-06-29
  • bring up staging
Week 13 (IAD) 2020-07-06
  • bring up staging
