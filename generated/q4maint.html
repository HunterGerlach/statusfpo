Infrastructure 2017 Q4 maintenance

During the week of 2017-12-04 - 2017-12-08, all Fedora Infrastructure servers get moved

This page gives information about when what boxes will be down, and when they are finished moving.

For every day, the work will start around 15:00 UTC (08:00 UTC-7) and end approximately at 00:00 UTC (17:00 UTC-7)

  • All hosts updated
  • Firmware updates for some hosts
Tuesday 2017-12-05
  • Development and Build servers being moved
Wednesday 2017-12-06
  • Production boxes moving
  • QA/Community boxes moving
Thursday 2017-12-07
  • Production boxes moving
  • Infrastructure Cloud moving
Friday 2017-12-08
  • Recabling/wiring
  • Missed updates
  • Wrap-up of work
