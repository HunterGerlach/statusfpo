Fedora Project Account System Migration

We are in the process of migrating to our new Account System. We strive to cause as little interruption as possible, but service availability during this outage isn't guaranteed. Find details about how this might affect you in the announcement on the mailing list.

This page is a manually updated emergency notification of known outages. If stuff is down but not shown, we probably don't know about it. Please open a ticket at our ticket system.
